About us

We are a research-driven startup dedicated to crafting pollution-preventing technologies as well as
advocating for the adoption of clean technology and sustainability practices.

At Greenovate Solutions we combat climate change by converting Industrial Emissions into Resources. We aim to capture Industrial CO2 emissions to utilize as a resource to alternate imports & production of LCO2. Leading to a carbon -ve & revenue +ve ecosystem.

Our Vision

Our goal is a carbon-negative, revenue-positive ecosystem by capturing emissions and producing liquid CO2 to reduce imports. Hence help small and medium scale industries achieve "Waste to Best for CO2 emissions.”

Our Mission

To capture and repurpose 500 tonnes of CO2 by December 2025 and 1 million tonnes by 2035. We support the IPCC’s goal to stay within the carbon budget and help as many medium and small-scale industries incorporate CCUS as possible.

The Founder

Mr. Ronak Mistry, a Chemical Engineer, founded Greenovate Solutions with a vision to drive sustainable innovation. With proven technical and collaborative skills, practical manufacturing experience, and expertise from R&D projects at Venture Center, CSIR-NCL, and SINE, IIT Bombay, he is dedicated to transforming industrial CO2 emissions into valuable resources.

“I wish to see a world where industrial CO2 emissions
are transformed into valuable resources, driving
sustainability and prosperity for all.”

Mr. Ronak Mistry

Our Journey

Where it all started..

A team of chemical and mechanical engineering students from MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune who share the same vision got together to undertake R&D project of developing concepts for miniaturizing Industrial CO2 mitigation technology. The project was being coordinated under the guidance of college professors and experts from research and industrial sectors.

Commissioning our first prototype

Towards the completion of our R&D, we were able to demonstrate 10KG/Day. CO2 capturing within our lab. We invited all our network & supporters for the past 2 years, along with various Industry leaders to showcase a live demonstration working of our first prototype.

Our first Pilot project

The first pilot project TubeCC will be tested in the third quarter of 2024. We have a target to conduct a minimum of 5 Pilot Projects by Dec 2024. Each of 5-10 TPD capacity across various different sectors & application.
