About Tube CC
Introducing the TubeCC, Greenovate's innovative Carbon Capture Technology unit. It captures carbon from point sources with high efficiency, enabling industries to utilize the CO2 beneficially. This revolutionary solution, developed through intense R&D, offers an effective way for industries to reduce their carbon footprint.
Technology
This cutting-edge technology not only helps reduce the environmental impact but also supports businesses in achieving their sustainability goals and comply with govt. and environmental targets. With its ease of integration and efficient performance, the TubeCC empowers industries to contribute to a cleaner, greener future.
Industries
At Greenovate Solutions we combat climate change by developing small scale, low expense Carbon Capture Systems for enabling Medium & Small Scale Industries (i.e. Textile, Fertilizers, Chemicals etc.) with CCUS.
